About Hyperclast

Building foundational tools for teams who value simplicity, speed, and ownership of their data.

Our Mission

At Hyperclast, we believe that the best tools are the ones that get out of your way. We're building foundational software for collaboration: fast, open, and built to last.

Our platform combines the simplicity of plain text with the power of real-time collaboration, built on modern foundations like CRDTs for conflict-free editing and a comprehensive API that gives you full control over your data.

Our Values

Speed

Performance isn't a feature, it's a requirement. Every interaction should feel instant.

Privacy

Your data belongs to you. We don't sell your information or train AI models on your content.

Open Source

Our core platform is open source. Inspect the code, self-host, or contribute to make it better.
